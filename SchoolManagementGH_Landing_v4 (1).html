Platform Features

Everything your school needs,
built into one system.

Replace notebooks, spreadsheets, and manual processes with a single platform designed for how Ghanaian schools actually work.

Fee Collection and Receipts
Record payments in seconds. Cash, Mobile Money, Bank Transfer, Cheque — every transaction receives a unique receipt number and is permanently recorded with the cashier name and date.
Automatic SMS to Parents
Parents receive an SMS receipt the moment a payment is recorded. Send bulk fee reminders to every parent with an outstanding balance in a single action.
Parent Self-Service Portal
Each parent receives a unique private link to check their child's fee balance and complete payment history — from any phone, anytime, without creating an account.
Financial Reports and Analytics
Instant per-class, per-term fee collection reports. Monthly collection trend charts. Export clean, professional reports to PDF or Excel with a single click.
Student Records Management
Complete student profiles with photos, class history, fee records, discounts, and guardian contact details. Import hundreds of students at once from Excel.
Staff Records and Payslips
Manage staff records, salaries, and SSNIT deductions. Generate professional, itemized monthly payslips automatically for every member of staff.
Levies and Other Charges
Track PTA levies, textbook fees, uniform fees, and exam levies completely separately from term school fees — each with their own payment records.
Academic Year Management
Start a new academic year with one action. Students are promoted automatically. All previous year data is archived and remains fully accessible whenever you need it.
Security and Full Audit Trail
Every action in the system is permanently logged — who recorded a payment, who edited a record, and exactly when. Complete transparency and accountability at all times.
