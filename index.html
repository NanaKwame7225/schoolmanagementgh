Platform Features

Everything your school needs,
in one place.

Replace notebooks, spreadsheets, and manual processes with a complete, professional digital management system.

💳
Fee Collection and Receipts
Record payments in seconds. Cash, MoMo, Bank Transfer, Cheque — every transaction gets a unique receipt number and is permanently recorded with date, cashier, and remarks.
📱
Automatic SMS to Parents
Parents receive an SMS receipt the moment a payment is recorded. Send bulk fee reminders to all parents with outstanding balances with one click.
👨‍👩‍👧
Parent Self-Service Portal
Share a unique private link with each parent. They check their child's fee balance and full payment history from any phone anytime — no app or login needed.
📊
Financial Reports and Analytics
Instant per-class, per-term fee collection reports. Monthly collection trend charts. Export professional reports to PDF or Excel with a single click.
👥
Student Records Management
Complete student profiles with photo, class, full fee history, discounts, and guardian contacts. Import hundreds of students at once from an Excel spreadsheet.
👨‍🏫
Staff and Payroll Management
Manage staff records, salaries, and SSNIT deductions. Generate professional, itemized monthly payslips automatically for every member of staff.
📦
Levies and Other Charges
Track PTA levies, textbook fees, uniform fees, exam levies, and other charges completely separately from term school fees with their own payment records.
🎓
Academic Year Management
Start a new academic year with one click. Students are automatically promoted to the next class. All previous year data remains fully accessible.
🔐
Security and Full Audit Trail
Every action in the system is permanently logged — who recorded a payment, who edited a record, and exactly when. Complete accountability at all times.
